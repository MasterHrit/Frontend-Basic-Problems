Apex Badminton Tournament

The game developed in British India from the earlier game of battledore and shuttlecock. The modern game of badminton developed in the mid-19th century among the British as a variant of the earlier game of battledore and shuttlecock.
Early on, the game was also known as Poona or Poonah after the garrison town of Pune, where it was particularly popular and where the first rules for the game were drawn up in 1873.
The game has also become a popular backyard sport in our country.

We organize Annual Apex Badminton Tournament for all of our students.
